Code Fish Cake

Description

Flavor cooked cod fillets with Parmesan cheese and garlic to create our cod cakes. Theyâ€™re a tasty way to use up leftover fish or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 1 pound cod fillets
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatoes
  2. Simmer and flake the fish
  3. Mix the fish, potatoes, and remaining ingredients together
  4. Form the mixture into cakes and fry
